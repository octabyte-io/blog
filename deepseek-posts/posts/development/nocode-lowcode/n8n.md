## What is N8N?

N8N (pronounced "n-eight-n") is a flexible, open-source workflow automation tool that allows you to connect various apps, services, and APIs to automate repetitive tasks. Whether you're integrating tools, automating data transfers, or building complex workflows, N8N provides a user-friendly interface to streamline your processes. It’s a powerful alternative to tools like Zapier or Make (formerly Integromat), with the added benefit of being open-source and highly customizable.
