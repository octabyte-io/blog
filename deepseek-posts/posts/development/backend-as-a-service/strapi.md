## What is Strapi?

Strapi is a leading open-source headless Content Management System (CMS) that empowers developers to build, manage, and deploy content-rich applications with ease. It’s highly customizable, API-first, and designed to work seamlessly with modern front-end frameworks like React, Vue, and Angular. Strapi is perfect for developers who want flexibility, scalability, and full control over their content structure.

## Why Use Strapi?

- **Flexibility:** Strapi’s headless architecture allows you to deliver content to any device or platform.
- **Customizable:** Easily create custom content types, APIs, and workflows tailored to your needs.
- **Developer-Friendly:** Built with developers in mind, Strapi offers a modern tech stack and extensive plugin ecosystem.
- **Scalable:** Whether you’re building a small blog or a large enterprise application, Strapi scales with your needs.
