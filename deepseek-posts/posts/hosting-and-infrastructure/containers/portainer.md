## What is Portainer?

Portainer is a lightweight, open-source container management tool designed to simplify the deployment, management, and monitoring of Docker and Kubernetes environments. With its intuitive web-based interface, Portainer makes it easy for developers and IT teams to manage containers, images, networks, and volumes without needing deep expertise in container orchestration.

Whether you're running a small-scale application or managing a complex microservices architecture, Portainer provides the tools you need to streamline your container workflows.

## Why Use Portainer?

1. **User-Friendly Interface:**  
   Portainer's web-based dashboard offers a simple and intuitive way to manage containers, making it accessible even for those new to containerization.
