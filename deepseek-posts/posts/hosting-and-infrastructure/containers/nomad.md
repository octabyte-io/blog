## What is HashiCorp Nomad?

HashiCorp Nomad is a flexible and highly efficient workload orchestrator designed to deploy and manage applications across on-premises and cloud environments. It supports containerized, non-containerized, and batch applications, making it a versatile tool for modern infrastructure. Nomad simplifies application deployment, scaling, and resource management, enabling teams to focus on building great software rather than managing infrastructure.

## Why Use Nomad?

- **Versatility:** Nomad supports a wide range of workloads, including Docker containers, standalone applications, and batch jobs.  
- **Scalability:** Easily scale applications up or down based on demand, ensuring optimal resource utilization.  
- **Simplicity:** Nomad’s declarative job files make it easy to define and deploy applications with minimal effort.  
- **Multi-Cloud Support:** Deploy applications across multiple cloud providers or on-premises environments seamlessly.
