## What is DockerRegistry?

DockerRegistry is a storage and distribution system for Docker images. It allows developers to store, manage, and deploy Docker container images in a centralized repository. Whether you're working on a small project or managing a large-scale application, DockerRegistry ensures that your containerized applications are easily accessible and version-controlled.

## Why is DockerRegistry Useful?

1. **Centralized Image Management:**  
   DockerRegistry provides a single source of truth for all your Docker images, making it easier to manage and distribute them across teams and environments.

2. **Version Control:**  
   With DockerRegistry, you can tag and version your Docker images, ensuring that you always have access to the right version of your application.

3. **Improved Collaboration:**  
   Teams can share and collaborate on Docker images seamlessly, reducing the risk of inconsistencies and improving productivity.

4. **Enhanced Security:**  
   DockerRegistry supports private repositories, ensuring that your images are only accessible to authorized users.
